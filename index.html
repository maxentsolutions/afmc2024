Welcome ! 

We invite you to join us at the 24th Australasian Fluid Mechanics Conference, to be held in Canberra, Australia in December 2024. This conference is being organised for the Australasian Fluid Mechanics Society, and brings together leading Australian, New Zealand and international scientists, engineers, educators and research students with common interests in fluid mechanics.

The city of Canberra offers a number of unique opportunities for this biannual meeting of the Australasian fluid mechanics community. The name Canberra is derived from Kambera, meaning "meeting place" in the local Ngunnawal language. As Australiaâ€™s meeting place, Canberra is home to - and brings together - the key decision-makers of the nation, including government, industry, the social sector and the media. The program of AFMC2024 will pay homage to these unique features, to provide maximum benefits to our community.

This event will provide excellent opportunities for social and scientific networking, to strengthen links between Australian, New Zealand and international communities in academia, industry and government.

The upcoming event will be the 24th in a successful series of Australasian Fluid Mechanics Conferences, and the first time hosted in Canberra. The proceedings of the previous AFMCs, dating back to 1962, may be found here on the website of the Australasian Fluid Mechanics Society.

The AFMC will be a family-friendly conference, so we encourage you to make your visit a family affair and enjoy all that Canberra and the ACT region can offer. The conference will be held with in-person attendance only.

We look forward to welcoming you to Canberra in December 2024 !
